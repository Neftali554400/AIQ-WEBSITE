Pricing questions
Do I pay monthly or once?
Once. You pay a single one-time fee per course and get lifetime access. There is no subscription, no renewal, and no hidden fees.
What's your refund policy?
We offer a full refund if you request it within 48 hours of purchase and have watched fewer than 2 lessons. Email hello@aiq-courses.com with your request.
Can I pay in Nigerian Naira?
Yes — all prices are in Nigerian Naira (₦) and payments are processed securely via Paystack, which supports cards, bank transfers and USSD.
Will course content be updated?
Yes. AI tools evolve fast and we update courses when major tools change. As a student you get all future updates to a course you've purchased at no extra cost.
Do I need any prior experience?
No. Most courses are built for complete beginners. If a course is intermediate level it's noted on the course page. All you need is curiosity and a device to learn on.
