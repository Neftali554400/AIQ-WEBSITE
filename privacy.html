Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it and your rights over it. We will never sell your data to third parties.

1. Who we are

AIQ operates the website and course platform available at aiq.ai. When this policy refers to "AIQ", "we", "us" or "our", it means the AIQ platform and its operators. For privacy enquiries, contact us at privacy@aiq.ai.

2. Data we collect

Account information

When you register, we collect your name and email address. If you sign up via Google or Facebook, we receive only the basic profile information you authorise through those platforms.

Usage data

We collect information about which courses you access, how far through each lesson you progress, and your activity within community features. This helps us improve our content and personalise your experience.

Technical data

We automatically collect basic technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device type and pages visited. This is used for security, analytics and to ensure the platform works properly on your device.

Communications

If you contact us by email or use community features, we retain those communications in order to help you.

3. How we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and operate the AIQ platform and courses
  • Track your learning progress and save your place in courses
  • Send course updates, new content notifications and occasional product emails (you can opt out any time)
  • Improve our courses and platform based on how people use them
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse or security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with any third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Legal basis (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, we process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract — to provide you with the service you signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — to improve our platform and keep it secure
  • Consent — for optional emails and communications

5. Third-party services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate AIQ:

  • Google Sign-In — for OAuth authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Facebook Login — for OAuth authentication. Governed by Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics — we use privacy-respecting analytics to understand platform usage in aggregate.

These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

6. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it longer.

7. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences and understand how our platform is used. We use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the platform to function (login sessions, security)
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use AIQ (aggregated, anonymised)
  • Preference cookies — remember your settings and progress

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may prevent some features from working.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights over your personal data:

  • The right to access a copy of the data we hold about you
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to delete your account and associated data
  • The right to object to or restrict certain processing
  • The right to data portability (receive your data in a machine-readable format)
  • The right to withdraw consent for optional communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@aiq.ai. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children

AIQ is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page and, for significant changes, notify you by email. Continued use of AIQ after changes are published means you accept the updated policy.

11. Contact us

If you have questions, concerns or requests relating to your privacy, please contact us at privacy@aiq.ai. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 5 working days.
